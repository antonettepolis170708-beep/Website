REFLECTION

This activity focused on implementing HTML forms and links, using the Palawan Express Pera Padala form as a practical example. I gained hands-on experience in creating various form input fields, such as text fields, dropdown menus, and radio buttons, to collect necessary information for a money transfer. I also learned how to use appropriate attributes for form validation and accessibility, ensuring a user-friendly experience. The activity reinforced my understanding of form submission and data handling. Additionally, I practiced creating different types of HTML links to connect the form to other relevant pages or resources, enhancing navigation. The challenges I encountered involved accurately replicating the Palawan Express form fields and implementing basic form validation to ensure data integrity. Overall, this exercise was crucial in mastering HTML forms and links, essential components for creating interactive and functional web applications.
