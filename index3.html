Theoritical Framework



This study is anchored on the organizational resilience theory by Vogus and Sutclifffe is understood as an organization that can anticipate, prepare for respond to and adpt to both gradual change and sudden discruptions to survive and thrive. Its about building a culture that can withstand and adapt to disruptions, changes, and challenges while maintaining its core functions and objectives. Reselient organizations are better equipped to navigate disruptive changes such as technological advancements shifting market dynamics and unnexpected crises (Vogus & Sutcliffe, 2007).

Given the lemited empirical research on resillience within organizational theory there are numerous oppurtunities fo future exploration of this cocept, especially in the context of small businesses. One possible explanation for organizational resillience is availability of slack resources such as finanacial reserves, human capital, or conceptual slack (Schulman,1993) wich provide organizations with the flexibility needed to absorb shocks and adapt to change. These slack resources are essential to an organizations ability to cope with unnexpected challenges such as those brougth by technological disruptions.

In relation to handicraft businesses facing technological challenges maintaining an up-to-date awareness of thier operational limits and availabe resources is critical.For handicraft businesses this margin could include to access technology, skilled labor fenancial flexibility, wich allows them to adapt to technological innovations without overextending themselves. In the context of this study the ability of handicraft businesses in Calbayog City to maintain a sufficient marginof resources will be crucial in their efforts to navigate the technological challenges they face. Operating too close to this margin particularly in an era of rapid technological change, could expose businesses to significant risks, emphasizing the need for effective coping strategies.

In support with the theory.The Dynamic Capabilities Theory proposed by Teece Pisano and Shuen (1997) focuses on an organization ability to integrate, develope, and reconfigure both internal and external resources and capabilities effectively respond to rapidly changing environments. This theory is particularly relevant to understanding the organizations such as handicraft businesses can adapt to technological challenges. It suggest that the businesses with strong dynamic capabilities are better positioned to recognize, absorb, and apply new technolgies or processes enabling them to stay competetive and resillient in a dynamic market.

This aligns with the Resillience Theory, wich highligths the importance of maintaining flexibility and sufficient slack resources to cope with unexpected disruptions. Both theories emphasie this need for businesses to not only survive but also to thrive in the face of challenges. In the context of the current study on the coping mechanisms of handicraft businesses towards technological challenges, Dynamic Capabilities Theory supports the idea that businesses must continously envolve by enhancing their technological capabilities are resource management. This is esential for overcoming technological hurdles adn remaining competitive, especially in an environment where digital transformation and innovation are constantly reshaping the industry.

By integrating the concept of dynamic capabilities with resilience, the study sugests that handicarft businesses, especially in Calbayog City must develop the capacity to continuously adapt technological changes. This involves building and reconfiguring bioth internal skills (such as digital literacy) and external resources (such as access to ecommerce platforms or supply chain emprovement) to ensure long-term businesses the study focus on adaptability but also reinforces the importance of having the resilience to manage and thrive despite technological disruptions.

The Organizational Resillience Theory and Dynamic Capabilities. Theory are both highly relevant to understanding how handicraft businesses cope with technological challenges. Organizational Resellience Theory emphasize the ability of businesses to absorb shocks and adapt to discruptions by maintaining slack resources such as fenancial reserves, skilled labor or technological flexibility wich is crucial for continuing operations during challenging times. In the case of handicraft businesses in Calbayog City,this theory helps explain how businesses manage limited resoirces and develop strategies to endure technological disruptions. On the other hand, Dynamic Capabilities Theory focuses on an organization ability to adapt and reconfigure internal an external resources in response to rapidly changing environments, highligthing the need for busineses to envolve through new technologies and innovations. For handicraft businesesses, this could involve adopting digital tools, enhancing prouction methods or tapping into e-commerce platforms to stay competetive.Together, these theories underscore the importance of adaptability and strategic flexibility in overcoming technological challenges. While resillience ensures that businesses can weather disruptions, dynamics capabilities enable them to actively transform and innovate ensuring long-term growth and survival in an increasingly digital world. This dual framework provides a comprehensive understanding of how handicraft businesses can not only cope with, but also thrive in, the face of technological change.
